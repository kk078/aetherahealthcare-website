FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services and process.

How quickly can you start managing our billing?

Most practices are fully operational with Aethera within 30-45 days. Our onboarding process is designed to be seamless with minimal disruption to your workflow.

What makes Aethera different from other billing companies?

We combine expert human billing professionals with advanced technology to deliver superior results. Our dedicated account teams understand the nuances of each specialty and work proactively to maximize your revenue.

How do you ensure HIPAA compliance?

We maintain comprehensive HIPAA compliance with annual training, business associate agreements, encrypted data transmission, and secure facilities. We undergo regular third-party security audits.

What reporting do you provide?

We provide real-time access to our provider portal with detailed analytics on collections, denials, AR days, and more. Monthly executive summaries and quarterly business reviews are included.

Do you work with all insurance types?

Yes, we handle Medicare, Medicaid, commercial insurance, and workers' compensation claims. Our team stays current with payer-specific requirements and updates.
