FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our reporting and analytics services.

How often do you provide reports?

We provide daily operational reports, weekly summary reports, monthly detailed dashboards, and quarterly business reviews. Custom reporting schedules can be arranged based on your needs.

Do you offer real-time data access?

Yes, our provider portal offers real-time access to claims status, payment information, and key performance metrics. Users can access data 24/7 with role-based security permissions.

Can you create custom reports?

Absolutely. We specialize in custom report development to meet specific practice needs. Our team works with you to understand requirements and deliver tailored reporting solutions within 3 business days.

How do you benchmark performance?

We compare your performance against specialty-specific industry benchmarks and our proprietary database of similar practices. This provides actionable insights for performance improvement.

What analytics capabilities do you offer?

Our analytics include trend analysis, predictive modeling, payer performance evaluation, provider productivity metrics, and financial forecasting to support strategic decision-making.
