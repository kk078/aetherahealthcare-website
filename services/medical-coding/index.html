FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our medical coding services.

What coding certifications do your coders hold?

Our coding team holds current certifications including Certified Professional Coder (CPC), Certified Coding Specialist (CCS), and specialty-specific credentials. All coders participate in ongoing education to stay current with annual guideline updates.

How do you ensure coding accuracy?

We employ a multi-step process including initial coding, quality review by senior coders, and compliance validation. All charts are reviewed for accuracy before claim submission, and we maintain detailed documentation of our coding decisions.

How quickly can you code our charts?

Our standard turnaround time is 24 hours for routine cases, with expedited 4-8 hour service available for urgent needs. Complex cases requiring additional research are completed within 48 hours.

Do you handle specialty-specific coding?

Yes, we have dedicated teams for each specialty with in-depth knowledge of specialty-specific coding requirements, common procedures, and payer expectations. Our coders regularly participate in specialty-focused training and education.

What happens during a coding audit?

During audits, we provide full transparency with complete documentation of our coding rationale. Our compliance team works collaboratively with auditors to ensure accurate reimbursement while maintaining compliance with all regulatory requirements.
